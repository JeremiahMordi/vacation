Yokoso minna-san...

Usually, I discuss the best vacation spots in the world. However, today I'll be discussing my favorite vacation spot which happens to be the great Greece.

As a person, I've always loved places filled with culture, myth, history yet with the comforts of the modern lifestyle. Now, Greece is all this rolled into one with a lot of extras. It has marvelous food, a nice atmosphere, loads of picturesque settings and many more. In the name of not clogging up the page, I have added a few links to further discuss my reasons for choosing Greece.
